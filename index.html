How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

From video to polished transcript in under a minute, all processed locally on your machine.

01

Paste or Upload

Drop a YouTube URL or any local video and audio file. We handle the rest.

02

AI Transcription

Whisper large-v3 turbo runs locally on your M-series chip. Fast, private, accurate.

03

Get Your Script

Clean text output with optional translation into 50+ languages via Google Translate.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for speed and simplicity. No cloud uploads, no API keys, no complexity.

▶

YouTube Support

Paste any YouTube URL. Audio is extracted automatically via yt-dlp with best quality selection.

📁

Local Files

Works with mp4, mkv, mov, mp3, wav, m4a and any format ffmpeg can handle.

🌐

Auto Language Detection

Whisper automatically identifies the spoken language or you can force a specific one.

📜

Translation

Translate transcripts into 50+ languages. Output both original and translated side by side.

⚡

Apple Silicon Native

MLX Whisper runs directly on your M-series GPU. No CUDA needed, blazing fast inference.

🔒

Fully Local

Everything runs on your machine. Your audio never leaves your device. Zero cloud dependency.
